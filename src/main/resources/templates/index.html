How To Use Sepura

SCRAPE

Simply scoop your leftover food waste into your sink and Sepura handles the rest. Your sink can be used freely with the ability to throw in all food waste

TURN ON

With a push of a button, solids are separated into an odourless, sealed collection centre in just seconds. The Sepura operates with a quiet hum and takes approximately 8 seconds to process all solid waste.

EMPTY

Simply check the LED lights that indicate the level of fullness and empty your collection centre when needed. Then, just dispose of the organic material in either your compost or your curb-side collection bin.
